Installation
------------------------------------------------
1. Log into your WordPress Admin.
2. Click Appearance.
3. Click Themes.
4. Click Add New.
5. Click Upload Theme.
6. Click Browse.
7. Select realestatepro.zip and click Open.
8. Click Install Now.
9. Click Activate.
10. At the top of the screen you will see a notice informing you to install 3 required plugins, Advanced Custom Fields, Site Origins Page Builder, and Site Origins Widget Bundle. Click 'Begin installing plugins'.
11. From the Install Required Plugins page for one by one for each of the three plugins click Install.
